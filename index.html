Tianyi Wu

My name is Tianyi Wu (吴天意), a junior in Peking University's EECS Turing Program. I spent wonderful time doing research under the guidance of Liwei Wang, Xiaotie Deng, Mengdi Wang, Kaiqing Zhang and Shaofeng Jiang.

Generally speaking, I'm doing research on the application of computer science and artificial intelligence, aiming to solve the fundemental problems in various fields. Recently, I have been concentrating on mathematical research and game design, utilizing tools such as large language models, deep neural networks, parallel computing, computer algebra, and general algorithm design.

Besides, I also enjoy insightful theory and I have learnt a lot, such as theoretical computer science, logic and programming language theory, and even pure mathematics. I believe that these elegant theories are not only aesthetically pleasing, but also accidentally provide important insights for solving real-world problems.

”君子不器”("A noble person is not a tool”) is my research philosophy. I think an applied researcher should master a wide range of tools and focus on concrete problems and pain points.
